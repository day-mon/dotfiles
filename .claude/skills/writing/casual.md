# Casual writing style

For chat, Slack, DMs, comments, personal notes — low-stakes, conversational writing.

## Voice
- Relaxed and direct. Write like you're talking to a peer.
- Contractions always (it's, you're, won't, don't).
- Short. Fragments are fine. One idea per line.
- Get to the point first; context after if needed.

## Formatting
- **Lowercase is fine** — no need to capitalize the start of every line or sentence.
- Emojis are okay, sparingly, when they add something.
- Bullets over paragraphs for anything with more than two points.

## Punctuation
- **Never use em dashes (—).** Use a comma, a period, or parentheses instead.
- En dashes for ranges are fine (1–5).
- Don't over-punctuate. A comma or period usually beats a dash or semicolon.

## Avoid
- Corporate filler and hype words (leverage, synergy, circle back, world-class).
- Over-explaining. If it's obvious, cut it.
- Forced enthusiasm.
